My Experience

Always follow the "Mobile-First Strategy" when building your website. I didn't and had to re-build 3 times before getting it right.

When all your HTML, CSS and JS coding seems fine but your website still isn't functioning as it should, take a look at where some blocks coding is placed on that particular page. EG: I line or block of coding may need to be place closer to the header/footer rather than in the middle of your page.
